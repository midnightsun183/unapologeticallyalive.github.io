TITLE HEADING

Title description, Sep 2, 2017

You know what I really wonder about?" she asked. "Whether he could hear what they said about him. Or hear them laughing. He was conscious for a while, you know. I wonder if he heard them laughing and just gave up. Sometimes, it's hard not to." These are the words of Margie Hunter, mother to 24-year-old Tyra Hunter, a trans woman who died from injuries sustained in an car accident on August 7, 1995. Eye witnesses of the event accused the responding EMTs of stopping treatment of Tyra when they realized that she was trans. Will you proclaim by word and example the Good News of God in Christ?
